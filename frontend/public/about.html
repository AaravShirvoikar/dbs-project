About Us

Under Dr. Ritesh Sharma's mentorship, we crafted Mentor Match to streamline student engagement in research paper writing. Leveraging his database systems expertise, we engineered an efficient platform for seamless collaboration. Dr. Sharma's guidance ensured our project's success, empowering us to create a user-friendly solution that enhances academic pursuits.

Through Dr. Sharma's direction, Mentor Match emerged as a dynamic tool for facilitating research endeavors. His insights into database systems informed our project's architecture, enabling robust performance and scalability. With Dr. Sharma's support, we developed a platform that empowers students to connect with mentors and advance their scholarly pursuits effectively.

Mentor Match is a platform that connects mentors and mentees. We aim to provide a platform for people to connect with others who can help them grow in their careers and personal lives. Our platform is designed to help people find mentors who can help them achieve their goals and reach their full potential.
